Carne Asada

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds flank or skirt steak
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • chopped avocado
  • Lime wedges

Steps

  1. Marinate the steak
  2. Preheat the grill
  3. Sear the steak
  4. Move the steak to the cool side of the grill
  5. Tent with foil and let rest
  6. Slice the steak across the grain
